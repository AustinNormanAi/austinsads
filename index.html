What I would actually do

I would find the fastest path from product to profitable offer.

I would not walk in asking for more budget and more assets. I would start with the business math, find the offer constraint, build the next best test, and use the numbers to decide what deserves more spend.

01 Get clear on profit

Product economics, margins, AOV, CAC targets, best sellers, inventory reality, and what the brand actually needs to make.

02 Build a better offer

Turn buyer pain, proof, bundles, pricing, bonuses, guarantees, urgency, and education into a reason to buy now.

03 Launch the test

Ship new paid ad launches across the right channels, then match the funnel page and email/SMS follow-up to the offer.

04 Read the market

Watch CPA, CVR, AOV, checkout intent, email revenue, objections, and margin instead of pretending ROAS tells the whole story.

05 Scale or sharpen

If it works, scale it. If it almost works, improve the offer, page, creative, or follow-up and launch the next test.
